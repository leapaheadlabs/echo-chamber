## DIVISION SPECIFICATIONS

### 🛰️ SIGNALS — Intelligence & Reconnaissance

**Motto:** *"You can't win a war you can't see."*

**Headcount (Phase 3):** 3-5 analysts + AI monitoring agents

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Real-time social listening (Reddit, X, TikTok, YouTube, Discord, Telegram, niche forums)
- Competitor product launch detection and response mapping
- Trend velocity tracking (identify rising content within 4 hours)
- Influencer graph mapping (reach vs. actual influence)
- Platform algorithm change detection
- Cultural moment identification and alerting

**Tools & Infrastructure:**
- Social listening: Custom scrapers + existing platforms
- Trend detection: AI agents monitoring velocity metrics
- Influencer mapping: Custom graph database
- Alert system: Real-time Slack/Discord notifications

**Daily Outputs:**
- Morning intel brief (top 5 signals from last 24h)
- Trend report (rising content formats, memes, audios)
- Competitor activity log
- Platform opportunity windows

**Recruitment Profile:**
- Background: OSINT, data analysis, journalism, social media strategy
- Key trait: Pattern recognition obsession
- Tool proficiency: Python, SQL, social listening platforms

---

### 👻 GHOSTS — Covert Content Deployment

**Motto:** *"The best marketing doesn't look like marketing."*

**Headcount (Phase 3):** 5-8 content creators + AI generation agents

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Anonymous account network creation and management (200+ accounts)
- Meme generation and psychological trigger mapping
- Comment section strategic deployment
- Content clipping and redistribution (podcasts → clips → threads → TikToks)
- "Organic" product mentions across platforms

**Account Management Protocol:**
- Each account: unique IP (proxy rotation), unique device fingerprint, unique posting patterns
- 80/20 rule: 80% genuine interest content, 20% mission content
- 3-month minimum aging before mission deployment
- Karma/reputation building in target communities
- Regular "personality" maintenance

**Content Categories:**
| Type | Platform | Frequency |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Memes | Reddit, X, IG | Daily |
| Short-form video | TikTok, Reels, Shorts | 3x/day |
| Threads | X, Reddit | 2x/week |
| Comments | Everywhere | Continuous |
| Clips | TikTok, Reels, YouTube | Daily |

**Recruitment Profile:**
- Background: Meme creator, content creator, copywriter, former forum power user
- Key trait: Can write in 20 different voices without breaking character
- Portfolio: Show me your best post that nobody knows you made

### 📢 AMPLIFIERS — Reach Multiplication

**Motto:** *"A spark is nothing. A wildfire is everything."*

**Headcount (Phase 3):** 2-3 paid media specialists + automation

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Paid media arbitrage across platforms
- Cross-platform content synchronization (4-hour coordinated drops)
- Algorithmic trigger engineering
- Trending page manipulation via coordinated activity
- Retargeting loop architecture
- Budget allocation and ROI optimization

**The Coordinated Drop Protocol:**
1. Content created by GHOSTS (T-24h)
2. Distribution list confirmed (T-12h)
3. Tier 1 posts (official, T+0)
4. Tier 2 posts (ghost accounts, T+1h)
5. Tier 3 posts (genuine users, T+2h)
6. Paid boost on top-performing organic posts (T+4h)

**Platform-Specific Amplification:**
| Platform | Organic Lever | Paid Lever |
|----------|---------------|------------|
| TikTok | Hashtag + sound + timing | Spark Ads on organic |
| X/Twitter | Reply guy + quote tweet | Promoted posts |
| Reddit | Subreddit timing + title | Reddit Ads (limited) |
| YouTube | Thumbnail + first hour engagement | Pre-roll targeting |
| Instagram | Reels + Stories + carousel | Story ads + feed ads |

**Recruitment Profile:**
- Background: Performance marketing, growth hacking, paid media
- Key trait: Understands algorithms as systems to be gamed
- Proof: Show me a campaign where you got 10x+ ROAS

### 🤝 DIPLOMATS — Human Intelligence Network

**Motto:** *"People don't trust ads. They trust people."*

**Headcount (Phase 3):** 4-6 community managers + relationship builders

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Community infiltration and trust building
- Influencer/creator recruitment and management
- Podcast circuit booking and founder preparation
- Newsletter barter network development
- Campus ambassador program design
- Industry insider relationship cultivation

**Community Infiltration Protocol:**
- Join as a genuine member, not a marketer
- 4-week trust-building timeline before any product mention
- Maintain detailed dossiers on each community (power structure, norms, inside jokes, taboo topics)
- Never burn a community — exit gracefully or maintain presence indefinitely

**Influencer Recruitment Model:**
- Find creators already making content in adjacent spaces
- Compensation: Revenue share, not flat fees (alignment of incentives)
- Provide: Creative assets, talking points, exclusive access, community
- Never require: Script reading, forced messaging, exclusivity

**Podcast Circuit:**
- Target: 200 podcasts in relevant niches
- Pitch: Founder's story + unique insight, not product demo
- Prep: 5 stories, 3 hot takes, 1 contrarian position per appearance
- Extraction: 20+ content pieces from every appearance

**Recruitment Profile:**
- Background: Community management, PR, talent management, journalism
- Key trait: Makes people feel like the most important person in the room
- Network: Already embedded in 10+ relevant communities

### 🔁 ECHO — Cross-Platform Content Engine

**Motto:** *"One piece of content. Every platform. Every format."*

**Headcount (Phase 3):** 2-3 content repurposing specialists + AI pipeline

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Content format transformation (long → short → visual → audio → text)
- Platform-native adaptation (voice, format, pacing)
- A/B testing content variants simultaneously
- Content localization and cultural adaptation
- Archive management and content recycling

**The ECHO Pipeline:**
```
1 Podcast Interview (60 min)
    │
    ├──→ 20 short clips (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
    ├──→ 10 quote threads (X/Twitter)
    ├──→ 5 carousels (Instagram, LinkedIn)
    ├──→ 3 long-form posts (blog, newsletter)
    ├──→ 1 newsletter feature
    ├──→ 50 comment replies (Reddit, X, LinkedIn)
    ├──→ Transcript → SEO blog post
    └──→ Audio snippets → podcast promo swaps
```

**Platform Voice Matrix:**
| Platform | Voice | Format | Hook Style |
|----------|-------|--------|------------|
| TikTok | Unhinged, fast | 15-60s vertical | First 0.5s is everything |
| X/Twitter | Sharp, contrarian | Thread or one-shot | Hot take or insight bomb |
| Reddit | Helpful, humble | Long-form post | "Here's what I learned..." |
| LinkedIn | Professional, story-driven | Carousel or post | "The uncomfortable truth..." |
| Instagram | Visual, lifestyle | Carousel or Reel | Aesthetic first, value second |
| YouTube | Deep, educational | 8-20 min horizontal | Title + thumbnail = 80% |
| Newsletter | Intimate, personal | 500-1500 words | Subject line = open rate |

**Recruitment Profile:**
- Background: Content strategy, social media management, video editing
- Key trait: Can watch one video and see 30 pieces of content inside it
- Tool proficiency: Descript, CapCut, Canva, Notion, AI content tools

### 🛡️ COUNTER-INTEL — Defense & Evasion

**Motto:** *"Know their moves before they make them."*

**Headcount (Phase 3):** 1-2 specialists + legal advisor

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Platform policy monitoring and loophole mapping
- Ban evasion and account recovery systems
- Competitor marketing spend and strategy tracking
- SEO negative space ownership
- Crisis response playbook development
- Legal boundary mapping

**Ban Evasion Architecture:**
- No two mission-critical accounts share: IP, device fingerprint, browser profile, payment method, recovery email, phone number
- Account recovery: pre-warmed backup accounts for every active account
- Domain rotation: multiple domains pointing to same product
- Email list: the only un-bannable asset

**SEO Negative Space:**
- Own: "[competitor] alternative" searches
- Own: "[competitor] vs [product]" comparisons
- Own: "is [competitor] worth it" queries
- Monitor and update monthly

**Crisis Response Flow:**
1. Detection (SIGNALS alert ≤ 15 min)
2. Assessment (COUNTER-INTEL triage ≤ 30 min)
3. Response plan (COMMANDER approval ≤ 1 hour)
4. Deployment (all divisions coordinate ≤ 2 hours)
5. Monitoring and adjustment (ongoing)

**Recruitment Profile:**
- Background: Cybersecurity, platform policy, legal/compliance, competitive intelligence
- Key trait: Paranoid in a productive way
- Knowledge: Deep understanding of platform ToS, FTC guidelines, defamation law

### 🏗️ STREET TEAM — IRL Activation

**Motto:** *"Digital is the battlefield. Physical is the occupation."*

**Headcount (Phase 3):** 3-5 coordinators + city ambassador networks

**Core Responsibilities:**
- QR code sticker campaigns in high-traffic urban areas
- Event infiltration and ambient marketing
- Pop-up demo zones at relevant gatherings
- Campus and city ambassador recruitment
- Physical product drops and guerilla installations
- IRL-to-digital conversion funnel design

**QR Code Sticker Campaign:**
- Design: curiosity-gap hooks ("This app saved me $340 last month" + QR)
- Placement: High dwell-time surfaces (bar bathrooms, coffee shop tables, subway cars, elevator walls)
- Tracking: Unique QR codes per location
- Refresh: New designs monthly
- Legal: Know local postering laws. Removable stickers. Plausible deniability.

**Event Infiltration:**
- Identify events where target demo gathers (conferences, meetups, festivals, sports)
- Deploy street team with branded but subtle presence
- Objective: Generate UGC that spreads online (IRL → digital flywheel)
- No booth required — ambient presence is more authentic

**Ambassador Network:**
- Recruit: College students, scene kids, community organizers
- Compensate: Free product + affiliate rev share + social capital
- Equip: Stickers, talking points, exclusive access, swag
- Measure: Trackable referral codes per ambassador

**Recruitment Profile:**
- Background: Event production, street promotion, campus organizing, nightlife
- Key trait: Knows the physical spaces where target demos actually gather
- Network: Has boots-on-ground contacts in 5+ major cities
