# TACTICS: TIKTOK PLAYBOOK

> *"TikTok is the only platform where a 15-second video from a 200-follower account can reach 2 million people. The algorithm is the great equalizer."*

---

## Platform Dynamics

- **Users:** 1B+ monthly active
- **Key trait:** Algorithm-driven, not follower-driven. Anyone can go viral.
- **Opportunity:** Lowest barrier to viral reach of any platform.
- **Risk:** Content ages fast. Trends die in 48-72 hours. Constant production required.

---

## The TikTok Algorithm (What We Know)

1. **First 200 views:** Shown to a test audience. Watch time + completion rate = everything.
2. **200-1000 views:** If metrics are good, expanded to broader audience.
3. **1000-10K:** Now competing in interest categories.
4. **10K+:** Viral acceleration if engagement stays high.

**The 0.5 Second Rule:** If you don't hook in the first 0.5 seconds, the scroll continues. The hook is 80% of success.

---

## Content Strategy

### Hook Archetypes (First 0.5 Seconds)

| Hook Type | Example | Best For |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Pattern Interrupt | Sudden movement, unexpected visual | Stopping the scroll |
| Controversy | "Hot take: [common belief] is completely wrong" | Engagement bait |
| Curiosity Gap | "I found something that changed everything..." | Watch time |
| Relatable Pain | "If you've ever struggled with [problem]..." | Niche targeting |
| Transformation | Before/after in first frame | Visual products |
| List/Value | "3 apps that save me 10 hours a week" | Utility products |

### Content Categories

1. **Product Demos** (20%) — Show the product solving a real problem. Not a feature walkthrough.
2. **Founder Story** (15%) — Why you built it. The frustration. The mission.
3. **Industry Commentary** (25%) — Hot takes on your industry. Establish expertise.
4. **User Wins** (20%) — Real users' results. Social proof.
5. **Behind the Scenes** (10%) — Building in public. Humanizes the brand.
6. **Trend Jacking** (10%) — Participating in trends with your product's angle.

---

## Account Network Strategy

### Main Account (Official Brand)
- Face of the company
- Founder-led content preferred
- Posts: 2-3x/day
- Voice: Authentic, opinionated, human

### Ghost Accounts (5-10 accounts)
- Each: unique niche, personality, content style
- Posts: 1-2x/day
- Product mentions: subtle, contextual, 1 in 20 posts
- Purpose: Create the appearance of independent enthusiasm

### User-Generated Content Seeding
- Send product to micro-creators (no contract, just free product)
- They post if they genuinely like it
- Repost the best UGC on main account

---

## Sound Strategy

- **Original audio:** Create sounds that others can use. If your audio goes viral, every video using it is free distribution.
- **Trending sounds:** Jump on trending sounds within 4 hours of emergence.
- **Voiceover:** Your own voice builds personal connection. Use it often.

---

## Hashtag Strategy

- 3-5 hashtags per post
- Mix: 1-2 broad (#tech, #startup), 2-3 niche (#indiehacker, #saasbuilding)
- Create a branded hashtag for community building
- Don't use #fyp — it doesn't work the way people think

---

## Posting Schedule

Optimal posting times (varies by audience — test everything):
- Morning: 7-9 AM (commute scrolling)
- Lunch: 12-2 PM (break scrolling)
- Evening: 7-10 PM (prime scrolling)
- Weekend: Later morning (10 AM-12 PM)

**Cadence:**
- Main account: 2-3 posts/day
- Ghost accounts: 1-2 posts/day
- Minimum viable: 1 post/day on main account

---

## Viral Engineering Protocol

1. **Content creation:** 5 variants of each concept (different hooks, same core)
2. **Simultaneous drop:** Post all 5 across main + ghost accounts within 1 hour
3. **Engagement priming:** First 30 minutes — ghost accounts comment, like, save, share
4. **Performance monitoring:** At 2 hours, identify the best performer
5. **Amplification:** Boost the best performer with Spark Ads
6. **Iteration:** Kill the losers, double down on winners

---

## Measurement

- **Watch time:** Average % of video watched (target: >50%)
- **Completion rate:** % who watch to end (target: >30%)
- **Engagement rate:** (likes + comments + shares + saves) / views (target: >5%)
- **Share rate:** Shares / views (target: >1% — this is the viral signal)
- **Follower conversion:** New followers / views

---

*TikTok rewards volume, speed, and authenticity. Post more than you think you should. Be weirder than you think you should. The algorithm loves what humans actually watch, not what marketers think they should watch.*
